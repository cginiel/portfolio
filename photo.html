observations

These are all photographs I’ve taken between 2014 and 2019. Some are shot on 35mm film, others are shot on my iPhone, and one is shot on my mirrorless DSLR. I like the different kinds of stillnesses they capture. Eyes fixed on clouds, a truck parked sideways in a driveway, still water, an unsuspecting bunny. These are just a few observations.

I like this photo of a rabbit in my backyard in the spring of 2019. I’m pretty sure I was making braised short ribs when I stepped outside to get some fresh air (my kitchen doesn’t have a range hood). I saw the rabbit munching on dandelions. Both of us were hungry. Anyway, I didn’t want to scare it so I zoomed in as far as I could. Then I zoomed in on the photo and took a screenshot of it. It looks like an impressionist painting.
