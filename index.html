about

My name is Cameron Giniel. I’m a master’s student at the University of Michigan School of Information, studying user experience research and design. But you won’t find any of that here—except for the fact that I built and designed this website for a class in my program. This website is for photos. I took all of the photos on this website, including the background images. I’ve been taking photographs with more or less artistic—expressive—intentions for about seven years. I don’t think they’ve ended up anywhere but in my friends’ iMessages or occasionally on my social media. I thought that this website would be a good repository for them since I’ve had so many lying around collecting (digital) dust for years.
